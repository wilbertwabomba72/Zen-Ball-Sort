Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Zen Ball Sort handles information when you use the game. Zen Ball Sort is a single-player color sorting puzzle game designed for offline play. We do not require you to create an account.

Information We Do Not Ask You To Provide

The game does not directly ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or payment information.

Local Game Data

Zen Ball Sort stores gameplay progress, unlocked levels, star ratings, move counts, music settings, and sound settings locally on your device. This local data is used to provide gameplay features and is not intentionally sent to the developer by the core game logic.

Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

The app may include third-party advertising, analytics, security, or fraud-prevention SDKs. These third-party services may process technical information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app activity, crash or diagnostic data, approximate region, network information, and ad interaction data in order to provide ads, measure performance, prevent fraud, and keep the app stable.

These third-party providers handle information according to their own privacy policies and technical practices. The developer does not directly control how those third-party SDKs process data once it is handled by their services.

App Tracking Transparency

When required by Apple policies, the app uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before tracking-related SDK initialization. If you deny tracking permission, advertising partners should not use the device advertising identifier for tracking across apps and websites.

Internet Access

Core gameplay is designed to work offline. Internet access may still be used by third-party SDKs for advertising, diagnostics, fraud prevention, attribution, or related service functionality.

Children's Privacy

Zen Ball Sort is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service used in the app, please contact the relevant service provider or email the developer at 508923216@qq.com.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the app data. Data processed by third-party SDKs is retained according to the privacy policies of those providers.

Your Choices

  • You can reset or delete local progress by deleting the app from your device.
  • You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can limit personalized advertising through iOS privacy settings where available.
  • You can turn music and sound effects on or off inside the game.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need technical support, please contact the developer at 508923216@qq.com.
